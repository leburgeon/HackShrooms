How to Play

Help your mushrooms grow and collect the shrinking mushrooms!

  • Click the mushroom to earn points.
  • Use points to purchase upgrades and skins.
  • Purchase 5 of an upgrade to unlock the next one.
  • Use the points you collect to upgrade your skin.
  • Save your progress and come back to your mushroom farm anytime.
